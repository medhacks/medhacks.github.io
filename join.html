Open Positions

Graphic Designer

MedHacks seeks a Graphic Designer eager to grow with a demanding and fast-paced organization. The Graphic Designer will be responsible for helping to shape and deliver projects with an emphasis on brand design for marketing deliverables. We want over-the-top creativity. Looking for proficiency in Adobe Photoshop, InDesign or Illustrator.

App Developer

MedHacks is going mobile! For the first time ever, we're hoping to create an app to help us scale along with the ever-growing size of the hackathon. We're looking for a coder interested in how to best improve the hacker experience at MedHacks with a mobile app, with profiency in Java, C++, or Swift. An interest in UI/UX is also a plus!

Web Developers

MedHacks is looking for talented web developers to help us refresh our online presence. Both frontend and backend coders are needed, and will be responsible for an overhaul of our website, as well as building an application portal, alongside our current tech team. We want motivated, driven individuals who are eager to learn. Looking for proficiency in HTML, CSS, JS, SQL, etc.

General Team Member

If you don't meet the specific qualifications for the other positions, don't worry! MedHacks is always looking for motivated individuals to join the team as a member of the Promotion, Logistics, or Outreach subteams. We're hoping for people with good teamwork, communication, and organizational skills, who are most importantly excited about the work that MedHacks is doing.
