The Executives

Emily Burnette, President

Emily, a junior from Palo Alto, California, is thrilled to serve as one of MedHacks' directors this year. Outside of MedHacks, she conducts biochemical research at the Bloomberg School of Public Health, volunteers with Alpha Phi Omega, and is a venture partner at Contrary Capital. She’s one of the very few prelaw students at Hopkins, and hopes to continue working within the intersection of government and law after completing her summer work with the US Patent and Trademark Office.

Theo Lee, Director

Theo believes that everyone has the right to high-quality, affordable healthcare. This summer he is interning at Blue Cross Blue Shield Association working to develop new technologies that improve healthcare access and effectiveness for all Americans. On campus, Theo is an associate at A-Level Capital and VP of communication of Alpha Kappa Psi. He’s also an avid soccer fan and loves boba.

Kathy Hu, Head of Community Engagement

Kathy's a sophomore Biomedical Engineering major with a minor in physics, originally from Davis, CA. She's passionate about the intersection of medicine, engineering, and technology, and loves working with kids. Apart from MedHacks, she participates in research, design team, and is a Hopkins tour guide! In the future, she hopes to be a surgeon who is also involved in the medical device industry.

Celine Arpornsuskant, Treasurer

Celine is a junior Biomedical Engineering major from Atlanta, GA. Outside of MedHacks, she is involved with design team and lion dance. Celine also has a passion for good food (a byproduct of growing up in a restaurant), so you can find her exploring farmers markets and going to restaurant openings on the weekends!
